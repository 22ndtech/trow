Trow - Image Management for Clusters

Manage and control the flow of container images through your Kubernetes cluster.

Features

  • Get a registry for your organisations images up-and-running quickly
  • Lock down the images that can run in your cluster
    • no more surprises from out-of-date and unverified images from the Docker Hub
  • Integrate with existing authentication and authorization solutions
  • Designed to be HA
  • Full audit log of operations
  • Advanced distribution options for quick deployment
  • Support for immutable tags and image streams
  • Open-source with enterprise subscription and add-on options
